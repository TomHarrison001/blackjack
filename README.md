# blackjack
Blackjack Unity Project

```
How to Play:

- Choose amount to bet
- Player and dealer are dealt 2 cards
- Player hits until they get 21+ or choose to stand / double down
- Dealer hits until they have 17+
- Player receives double their bet if they win and only their bet if they draw
- Make as much money as you can in 5 rounds
```
